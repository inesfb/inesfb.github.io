Hi! My name is Inés Fernández Barhumi and I am an undergraduate computational social science student at University College Dublin. I am interested in public policy, data science, and effective altruism. I am one of the leads of Effective Altruism Ireland and have done research for organizations including The UBI Center, the Alliance to Feed the Earth in Disasters, and charity evaluator SoGive.


Here is what I am up to currently (inspired by the "now page"):

  • Setting up up EA UCD through CEA's University Groups Accelerator Program.
  • Leading a project at SoGive evaluating No More Pandemics, a grassroots organization focused on incresing pandemic preparedness.
